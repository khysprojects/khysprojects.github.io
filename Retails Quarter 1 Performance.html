Project Title One

This project analyzes sales performance and highlights revenue trends, top products, and store performance.

Dashboard

Q1 Performance

Key Insights

  • Total revenue for Q1 reached £785.4K.
  • The steepest growth occurred between 15 and 19 March, when revenue jumped from £5.1K to £28.2K.
  • Wednesday was the best-performing day with highest revenue peak (£30.0K).
  • Despite overall growth, revenue fluctuated significantly throughout the quarter.

Recommendations

  • Plan more consistent sales and marketing activities to reduce revenue fluctuations.
  • Apply successful tactics from high-growth periods to improve performance during slower weeks.
  • Investigate why Thursdays and Fridays underperform and test targeted offers to boost sales on these days.
